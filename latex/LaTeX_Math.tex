\section*{Math Formula}
Math Formulas can be done shorthand or long hand.

Short (text inline):
$\forall x \in X, \quad \exists y \leq \epsilon$

Long (text separated and indented):
\begin{displaymath}
\forall x \in X, \quad \exists y \leq \epsilon
\end{displaymath}
